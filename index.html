🎭 Family Feud Rules

  • Two teams of 4 players each.
  • Teams decide player order before the game starts.
  • Each round begins with a Face-Off between one player per team.
  • Three survey questions are asked per face-off.
  • Points are awarded based on survey popularity rankings.
  • The team with the highest total score at the end wins.

🎬 Green Screen Rules

  • Each member participates individually; scores are combined for team total.
  • Participants are blindfolded throughout their turn.
  • A muted clip plays in the background for the audience.
  • Judges provide verbal clues to guide the participant.
  • Each participant gets 3 minutes to perform.
  • Judges' decisions are final and binding.
